Pizza

An easy Pizza recipe you should try

Spread pizza dough with tomato sauce, top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings, then bake at 475°F (245°C) for 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add your favorite toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.).
  6. Season with oregano, basil, or chili flakes.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
